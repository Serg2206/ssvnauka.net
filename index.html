How the consultation works

Before the visit

  • Send a short description of your concern
  • Share any test results or medical documents available
  • Indicate whether the case is urgent or routine

During the consultation

  • Discuss diagnosis, treatment options, and next steps
  • Clarify expected recovery and follow-up needs
  • Receive clear guidance on further care

How surgery works

Before surgery

  • Review medical history and diagnostic findings
  • Discuss the planned surgical approach and expected benefits
  • Prepare for preoperative instructions and recovery planning

After surgery

  • Monitor recovery and postoperative care
  • Coordinate follow-up visits and wound management
  • Support a safe return to daily activity

What to do in an emergency

Urgent symptoms

  • Severe abdominal pain or sudden worsening of symptoms
  • Fever, vomiting, or signs of infection
  • Any sudden change after recent surgery or treatment

What to do next

  • Contact the office as soon as possible
  • Describe the symptoms clearly and include timing
  • Seek urgent medical attention if symptoms are severe

How to prepare for surgery

Before your procedure

  • Collect recent test results and medical records
  • Follow any preoperative instructions provided by the office
  • Ask about medication changes, fasting, or recovery expectations

On the day

  • Arrive on time and bring any required documents
  • Inform the team about allergies, medications, or recent symptoms
  • Prepare for a short recovery period after the procedure
